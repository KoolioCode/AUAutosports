About

AUAutosport making a difference

Our club is broken into different segments: engineering and business teams. The engineering team focuses on maintaining our 2000 BMW E46 and designing parts to enhance the performance of our car. Students learn how to drive a manual tramission car, and how to use COmputer Aided Design (CAD) to design more efficient and effective parts for the BMW.
